Our ambition


With years of experience as both gamers and game developers we all know how frustrating it can be when a finished game doesn’t seem to live up to expectations. As for the development side it’s hard to gather data when millions of people cry for change in their own way. As with many things the discussion can become very polarised and even though the internet has grown bigger and bigger, the gap between developer and consumer grows with it.
We aim to change that!

After partnering up with multiple industry leading game developers and publishers we’ve created a beta testing program that aims to give the casual gamer an insight into the development process of their favorite franchises as well as providing the developer with first hand feedback before it’s too late!

Here you’ll find a form where you as a gamer can apply to be a part of our exciting program.
