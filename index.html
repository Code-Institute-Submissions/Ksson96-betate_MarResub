Our ambition


With years of experience as both gamers and game developers we all know how frustrating it can be when a finished game doesn’t live up to expectations. As with many things feedback can become very polarised and even though the internet has grown bigger, the gap between developer and consumer has grown alongside it. This is what we aim to change!

After partnering up with multiple industry leading game developers and publishers we’ve created a beta testing program that aims to give the casual gamer an insight into the development process of their favorite franchises as well as providing the developer with first hand feedback before it’s too late!
